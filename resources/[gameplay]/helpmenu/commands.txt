-------------------------------> Mods <-------------------------------

/blocker <name> <hours>
For example the following command will mark me as blocker for 2 hours:
/blocker Bierbuikje 2

/blockers
Shows an overview of all players in blocker mode

/k <name> <reason>
Blow up another player who camps or is bugged in Shooter or DD mode.

/mute <name> <reason> <seconds>
For example the following command mutes me for 30 minutes:
/mute Bierbuikje reason 1800

/unmute <name>
Unmutes a muted player.

/votekick <name> <reason>
Starts a votekick against a player.
